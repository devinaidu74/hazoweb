HAZO – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hazo, a social media platform that allows users to share and view content. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is managed responsibly and transparently.

Age Restriction: We do not allow children under the age of 13 to use our service. Only individuals born after December 31, 2010, are permitted to create accounts.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you sign up or interact with our app:

  • Username
  • First Name, Last Name, and Date of Birth
  • Email and Encrypted Password (bcrypt) – we cannot view your password
  • Profile Picture
  • Uploaded Posts (images/videos)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to provide essential features like user authentication, content sharing, and personalization. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

Users can report inappropriate content for review and potential removal to maintain a safe community.

Uploaded posts (images and videos) are visible to all users within the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored in MongoDB. Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt and are not accessible by anyone.

We use the following infrastructure:

  • AWS S3 – For secure storage and retrieval of user-uploaded media (images/videos)
  • AWS Elastic Beanstalk – For deploying and hosting our backend server and APIs
  • Nodemailer – To send one-time passwords (OTP) to user email during registration

These services follow industry best practices for encryption, privacy, and secure data handling.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request account deletion

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@hazo.co.in.

Users may delete their accounts, which removes their data and content from our platform.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AWS S3: For media hosting and delivery
  • AWS Elastic Beanstalk: For application hosting
  • Nodemailer: For sending OTPs via email (we do not store email content)

These services do not have access to your personal data like email passwords or profile details.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is active or as required for legal, regulatory, or operational purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If significant changes are made, users will be notified through the app or by email.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, reach out to us:
